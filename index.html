DEBATE By Mr. Confidence, Mrs. Jovita & Mr. Oscar

MOTION: LEADERS ARE BORN, NOT MADE

Point 1: Natural Leadership Traits Cannot Be Taught

Some people naturally command respect without asking for it.
From childhood, certain individuals:

  • Take initiative.
  • Organize games.
  • Solve disputes.
  • Influence their peers.
  • Assume responsibility naturally.
These characteristics appear long before formal education or leadership training. Argument: Leadership training can polish these qualities, but it cannot create them from nothing. Quote: "You can polish a diamond, but you cannot polish a stone into a diamond."
